Ultimate Carbonara

Description

The ultimate Creamy Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 100 g of pancetta or bacon
  • 50 g of pecornio
  • 50 g of parmesan
  • 3 large eggs, yolks only
  • 300-350g of spaghetti or any pasta
  • cloves of garlic
  • unsalted butter
  • salt and freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Finely chop the panchetta and cook them low heat to render out the fat
  3. Beat the 3 yolks and mix with cheese
  4. Fry the garlic with the butter and pancehtta
  5. When the pasta is almost done, transfer it to the pan and lower the heat
  6. Mix the egg/cheese mixture with the pasta with the heat almost off
  7. Add extra pasta water for consistency and season with salt and pepper
