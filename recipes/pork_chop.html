The Best Pork Chop

Description

Juicy and Tender Porkchops

Ingredients

  • 4 Porkchops, 1 inch thick, 6-7 ounces each
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of all purpose flour
  • 1 tspn chili powder
  • 1 tspn garlic powder
  • 1 tspn onion powder
  • 1 tspn of smoked paprika
  • neutral oil
  • 1 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 tbspn of apple cider vinegar
  • 1-2 tspn of honey or brown sugar
  • half a stick of butter
  • freshly chopped parsley / other herbs

Steps

  1. Make the spice rub, mix the flour, chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika and season.
  2. Dry the pork chops with a paper towel, then rub the spice with oil on the pork chops
  3. Cook until golden 2-3 mins each side
  4. Reduce the heat to low, transfer the chops to a plate
  5. Add the stock, vinegar, sugar to the pan
  6. Taste and season with salt until its reduced to half.
  7. Turn the heat off and with residual heat, add butter and parsely
  8. Slide the pork chops and the juices back to the pan and serve.
